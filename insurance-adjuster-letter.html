What This Is

Adjuster letters often explain claim decisions, request information, or outline the insurer’s position. These letters may sound definitive even when further discussion or documentation is still possible.

Understanding the intent behind the adjuster’s wording is critical before responding.

What Happens If You Ignore It

  • The insurer may move forward without your input
  • Claim status may be closed or limited
  • Opportunities to clarify or dispute may be lost
